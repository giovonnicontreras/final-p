Our mission

FitForge blends programming thinking with fitness: modular routines, repeatable patterns, and clear structure. It is designed for students, beginners, and anyone who wants workouts that are easy to follow and improve over time.

This site was built by Gio Contreras as a project for a computer programming class.

Why it stands out

  • Mobile-first layout with a consistent blue color theme.
  • Reusable components (cards, hero, navigation) similar to reusable functions.
  • Lightweight JavaScript for animations, navigation, and a workout carousel.
